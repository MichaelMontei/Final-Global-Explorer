GLOBAL EXPLORER

This page is not about 'how Global Explorer works' (there's a dedicated one here, in case you were wondering), it's more about giving you a sense of what Global Explorer is - or who we are. Global Explorer was born to connect people, cultures and stories through that magical activity that we call 'travelling'. So we group people together and send them to live incredible adventures around the globe. Some say that Global Explorer changed their lives, some others that they will never take part in a group tour ever again - we're not for everyone, but better try than regret, right?

Our business is profoundly human: it’s built on relationships and interactions - not only while we're travelling. We plan many events throughout the year to meet and engage our community and enable our Global Explorer and Travel Coordinators to share their stories with one another. Every day we get to see the impact our work has and we’re reminded that we earn a living making connections and changing lives. Not many companies can say that. This is our “why” - the reason we come to work every day - and we consider it a privilege.
